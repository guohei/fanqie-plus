# Fanqie Platform Strategy

Use this reference for projects intended for 番茄小说 or similar mobile free-reading platforms.

## Core Reading Experience

Fanqie-oriented prose should be easy to scan on a phone:

- Short paragraphs, usually 1-4 lines.
- Clear scene movement and concrete stakes.
- Early conflict, not delayed background explanation.
- Frequent emotional feedback: grievance, relief, curiosity, sweetness, tension, awe.
- Chapter endings that create a next-click reason.

## Golden Three Chapters

### Chapter 1: Immediate Hook

Required:

- Lead with conflict, danger, humiliation, reversal, secret, or abnormal behavior.
- Introduce protagonist and immediate trouble quickly.
- Avoid long setting explanation.
- Show the unique premise or its first pressure point.

### Chapter 2: Goal And Obstacle

Required:

- Give the protagonist a visible short-term goal.
- Make the obstacle specific: person, deadline, rule, money, status, survival risk.
- Reveal the system/gold finger/world rule through action, not exposition.

### Chapter 3: First Payoff

Required:

- Deliver a small win, reversal, face-slap, emotional sweetness, clue, or capability proof.
- Connect payoff to the first two chapters' conflict.
- End with a strong hook: danger, reveal, invitation, new enemy, bigger promise.

## Poison Points To Check

- Protagonist passive for too long.
- Protagonist acts stupidly only to keep the plot alive.
- A misunderstanding could be solved by one normal sentence but is artificially prolonged.
- Important insult or threat receives no satisfying response.
- Romance betrayal or humiliation is handled casually.
- Gold finger has no limitation or no reader-visible payoff.
- Supporting cast exists only to praise the protagonist.
- Supporting cast exists only to die, praise, deliver exposition, or hand over resources.
- The protagonist forgives enemies without cost, leverage, or reader-satisfying reason.
- The story applies different moral rules to the protagonist and everyone else without acknowledging it.
- Long explanations replace scenes.
- Core promise in title/introduction is absent from the opening chapters.

When a poison point appears, repair the cause rather than masking it with narration: give the protagonist a rational constraint, let characters speak the obvious sentence, add cost to forgiveness, or turn a tool character into someone with a desire and tactic.

## Audit And Compliance

Before publishing or checkpoint review, also read `platform-compliance.md`.

Practical rule: chapter正文 should contain no URLs, contact methods, group invites, QR-code prompts, off-platform diversion, ad copy, private transaction guidance, piracy links, or platform-abuse instructions. Treat these as blocking issues even if the surrounding prose is otherwise good.

## Mobile Prose Standards

- Prefer concrete action over abstract conclusion.
- Let dialogue carry conflict.
- Use sensory detail sparingly but specifically.
- Keep chapter metadata out of正文.
- Preserve one clear emotional target per scene.
